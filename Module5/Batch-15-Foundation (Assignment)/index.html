Our Donation Plan

People are experiencing a variety of issues during the flood. We can't solve every problem. However, our aim is to give them with food, clothing, and medication. In this case, they are the most essential requirements.

Cloth for people

During a flood, the majority of people endure a severe food scarcity. That is why we are attempting to serve foods for them.

Cloth for people

People have a cloth issue during a flood flash. Their house gets flooded as a result of the flood. That is why we are attempting to offer some clothing for them as well.

Medicine for people

Another critical aspect is medicine. During a flood, medical supplies is disrupted. That is why we are attempting to offer some clothing for them as well.
